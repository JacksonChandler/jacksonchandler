Hallmark IT Hackathon Summer 2019

Pocket Identifier

Worked in a small team to develop a image recognition application to identify card pockets in card racks. Specifically looking for pockets that hand only envelopes, empty pocket with a pocket identifier (The card the says "Mother's Day, Birthday, etc"), and an empty pocket without a pocket identifier or an envelope. After two working days, tagging thousands of images, and hours of training we were able to identify with about 73% accuracy. Several challenges were encountered including the time to tage that many images, the variety of cards confusing the algorithm on what a card is, and finding a empty pocket with no identifier because the plastic behind it is clear and just showed another card. Technologies used: Azure Cognitive Services and CustomVision.ai.
